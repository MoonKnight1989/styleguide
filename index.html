What is a brand?

This is who we are. Our brand is the first thing that people interact with when they discover us. It also extends deeper than just colours and logos, it is how we behave online and offline, from the words we speak to the attitude we convey.

This is why it is important to uphold and protect at all costs. Please familiarise yourself with the assets in this guide so that you can help us maintain a consistent brand across all channels of the business world.

A message from marketing

The logos, colours, messaging and attributes in this guide have been carefully crafted by our marketing team so please be sure to always follow them. If you need a branded asset for a sales pitch, social media post or exhibition, please let marketing know and we will ensure it is well branded and professional.

DO NOT attempt to make assets yourself unless approved to do so by marketing. You can request assets here:

Philosophy

The services and products we are building can be very tech heavy and complex, therefore it is essential we remain clear and concise. Our content, design and imagery/video should compliment each other, working together to deliver a meaningful message to the public, especially our users.

Our Mission

“To provide superior quality letting services that tenants will recommend to their friends and landlords prefer for their properties. To use innovative technology, data-driven decision making and expert knowledge of the market to improve the property letting experience”

Our Vision

“A faster, more transparent and efficient letting experience for tenants and landlords across the UK”

Our Colours

These colours have been chosen as they are modern, trustworthy colours that represent the vision of Oasis Living. Blues are our main colour with brighter complimentary colours for CTAs, accents and highlights. Lighter blues can be used for button hover colours and design accents. Black and greys are for text and shading.
